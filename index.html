Why Recycle

Conservation of Resources
With recycling we don't have to use up more and more of Earth's finite resources.
It saves Energy

It takes less energy to recycle something, than to make new raw materials. As recycling technology advances, this gap will grow bigger and bigger.

Reduce waste
Recycling reduces the waste. It doesn't mean we should be making this much of it, but at least we can reuse some and reduce the impact of waste.
Protect animals
Improper disposal of waste can harm wildlife and ecosystems. Especially in the oceans many animals suffer or die each year.

How to Recycle

Curbside Recycling
Many areas offer curbside recycling programs where you can throw away recyclable materials in designated bins. Typically you can dispose of paper, cardboard, glass, plastic, and metal. Check local government website for locations.
Drop-Off Recycling Centers
If you can't use curbside recycling bins, wether they are too far away, or you have special waste, you might want to check out drop off recycling centers.
Specialized Recycling
Some items, like electronics, batteries, and household hazardous waste, require specialized recycling. For these you have to drop them off at specified locations.
Composting
You can compost your organic waste, which not only will provide you with good soil, but can also save money waste disposal costs.
